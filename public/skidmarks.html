Skidmarks

Mission: To bring forward women's voices - especially middle aged and older women.
Current effort: "Skidmarks" - A two act stage-play about a middle aged 'sandwich generation' woman who is trying to balance both helping her adult children, and her aging parents while trying to continue to develop a life for herself apart from family needs.

"Skidmarks": The Sandwich Generation Dilemma. A play by Barb Slocum. Join us for a scene-reading of "Skidmarks": A sandwich Generation Dilemma A stage-play by Barb Slocum with playwright discussion following.

The first Friday of April at Orion Beauty and Balance on Gorgeous Washington Street in Downtown Binghamton.
