SLP/AUDIOLOGY DELEGATION TO CHINA/TIBET - JUNE 1, JUNE 11 2011

I was privileged to be a member of the SLP/Audiology - People to People Ambassadors program in June 2011. Our trip to China/Tibet was both professional and cultural. It took 10 flights and 12 days to complete the trip and it was awesome! Both China and Tibet are more modern, for the most part, than I expected them to be. It was invigorating to submerge myself in cultures so different from my own – to learn from them and to learn about myself as I absorbed everything around me.
